Are you ready to achieve your fitness goals?

Experienced Personal Trainer

I am Connor Golding, a personal trainer based in the Bristol area with over 6 years of experience in both one to one, and group training. Fitness is my passion and I specialise in helping others to push themselves and achieve the goals that they want to achieve, all while having a good time.

Exercise shouldn’t be something you dread, it should be something you look forward to! I work around your schedule to provide fun, challenging and unique workout structures built for you.

Qualifications: Level 3 CYQ Personal Training, Level 3 BTEC Sports Exercise & Development, Level 2 Gym Instructing, First Aid

Training that works for you

I have built up my experience over the world, having lived in Vancouver, Canada for 2 years, and the continuing experiences keep my training fresh. I love the outdoors and it really is the perfect gym, so come summer time I use it as much as I can! Bodyweight exercises are brilliant.

I understand that life can get busy, so I can bring my services to you to help you work out around your lifestyle.

Nutrition

Nutrition is over half the battle, if you don’t eat right you won’t see the results you want. I will help you build the ideal nutrition plan to go along with your training. You can make delicious tasting food with fresh ingredients that will benefit you in your daily routines.

The ideal nutrition plan doesn’t have to be the most strict in the world though, as long as you moderate. You don’t have to give up your wine completely just cut it down to a treat.
